## Packages
framer-motion | Essential for the smooth scroll animations and modern reveal effects requested for the portfolio.
react-hook-form | Form state management for the contact form
@hookform/resolvers | Zod resolver for form validation

## Notes
- The portfolio is designed as a single-page scrolling experience with a dark, tech-focused aesthetic.
- The contact form submits to the `/api/messages` endpoint defined in the backend routes.
- Tailwind config should include `fontFamily: { display: ["var(--font-display)"], body: ["var(--font-body)"], mono: ["var(--font-mono)"] }`
